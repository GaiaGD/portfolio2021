My name is Gaia, I am a Digital Designer and Front-End developer. I have more than eight years of experience and a solid background in Luxury and Advertising and all things digital: Websites, Apps, and not only.

I've done my Bachelor's degree in Communication Design at Politecnico di Milano and a Master of Arts in Communication Design at Central Saint Martins, University of the Arts in London, with a dissertation on the psychological aspect behind luxury consumption.
