AIRPLANEMODE

You’re on your way to the airport: wouldn’t it be great to have a “smart” ticket that tells you what is your gate numer, when is your flight, if you’ll face delays?
And maybe even gives you the weather condition, the arrival time and terminal? All in the same place?
This is why I‘ve conceptualised, designed and coded AIPLANEMODE: Entering your flight code, it generates an AJAX request from the form and fetches flightstats.com API.
Final touch: the background colour change according to the time of the day. Have a safe journey!
